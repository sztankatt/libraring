Our team

One of the most tiring duties of a university student is gathering his/her course related books. Every member of our team study as a university student and all of us know that finding and buying a rear book during a semester is one of the most complicated and expensive hardships that a student often encounters during his/her studies.

Therefore we created a platform which opens a new online market with infinite possibilities to exchange any books you may need during your academic years.

so get Libraring!

About the project

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Libraing is a free and online market where users can buy and resell their textbooks and/or any course related material. The website is designed to provide a university specific, dynamic and online service for students to swiftly trade their unwanted books with other users regardless of university or course as it happens on a joint platform.{% endblocktrans %}
